# Quick Start: Admin Shareable Links

## For Admin (cgumucio93@gmail.com)

### Creating a Shareable Link

1. **Login**: https://qr-transfers.web.app/login
   - Use your admin email: cgumucio93@gmail.com

2. **Go to Add Account**: Click "Agregar Cuenta" or visit `/add-account`

3. **Enable Shareable Mode**: 
   - You'll see a toggle: "Crear link compartible (para pagos en efectivo)"
   - Turn it **ON**

4. **Fill Client Details**:
   ```
   Nombre Completo: [Client's full name]
   RUT: [Without dots or dash, e.g., 181055049]
   Banco o Plataforma: [Select from dropdown]
   Tipo de Cuenta: [Select type]
   Número de Cuenta: [Account number]
   Email: [Client's email]
   ```

5. **Save**: Click "Guardar Cuenta"

6. **Copy Link**: A dialog appears with the shareable link
   - Format: `https://qr-transfers.web.app/claim/{unique-token}`
   - Click the copy icon to copy to clipboard

7. **Share**: Send the link to your client via:
   - WhatsApp: "Aquí está tu código QR: [paste link]"
   - Email
   - SMS
   - Any messaging app

### Normal Account (For Yourself)

- **Leave toggle OFF** when creating your own accounts
- These stay private in your dashboard
- Accessible only when you're logged in

## For Clients (Anyone with Link)

### Accessing Your QR Code

1. **Open Link**: Click the link received from admin
   - No login required
   - No account needed
   - Works on any device

2. **View Your Info**:
   - Full name
   - RUT
   - Bank/platform
   - Account type
   - Account number
   - Email

3. **Get QR Code**:
   - **Download QR**: Click "Descargar QR" → saves PNG image
   - **Copy Data**: Click "Copiar Datos" → copies to clipboard

4. **Use Anytime**: 
   - Save the link
   - Visit whenever needed
   - Link doesn't expire

### Sharing Your QR

When someone needs to pay you:
- Show them your QR code (from downloaded image)
- They scan it with their phone camera
- Your bank details appear on their screen
- They can copy and use for transfer
